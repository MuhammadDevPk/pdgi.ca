Faster Bookings, Built for Brokers

Give brokers and carriers a streamlined workflow: real-time load visibility, organized communication, and faster PODs to speed up payment cycles.

PDGI helps brokers manage capacity and documentation while minimizing friction. Brokers get accurate load statuses, driver-supplied PODs and photos, and a single place to track load progress — reducing surprise delays and disputes.

Real-time Load Visibility

  • • Live status updates (Assigned → Picked up → Delivered)
  • • Optional live GPS location sharing for progress tracking
  • • ETA updates and automated exception flags

Organized Communication

  • • Load-based chat threads keeping everything in context
  • • Attach photos and evidence directly to the load
  • • Centralized audit trail for all messages and photos

Document & POD Management

  • • Drivers upload PODs/BOLs directly in-app
  • • Auto-attach documents to the correct load record
  • • Timestamped photos and signature capture

Faster Invoicing

  • • Generate invoices the moment documents are uploaded
  • • Cut claim disputes with indisputable photo evidence
  • • Expedited payment cycles for brokers and carriers

Broker + Carrier Workflow

1. Book Load

Broker posts or books a load through the platform.

2. Accept & Start

Carrier accepts and driver receives load details in the app.

3. Upload POD

Driver updates status and uploads POD at the destination.

4. Instant Settle

Broker reviews POD, marks settled, and triggers invoicing.

Broker FAQ

Can I see driver locations without violating privacy?

Location sharing is configurable. Fleets can enable work-hours-only tracking or opt-in per load to meet privacy and policy needs.

Can brokers upload documents from their systems?

Yes — brokers can upload contracts or matching docs via the broker portal and tie them to loads.

Are broker and carrier communications stored?

Yes. All load-related messages, photos, and documents are stored and linked to the load for audit and dispute resolution.
