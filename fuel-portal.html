Card Management

Card Management Built for Fleets

Issue fuel cards in minutes, assign them to drivers, and stay in full control. Set rules per card, manage permissions, and instantly take action when something changes — all from one dashboard.

  • Create and assign cards to drivers or vehicles
  • Set daily/weekly spend limits and purchase rules
  • Lock, unlock, or replace cards instantly
  • Role-based access for dispatch, admin, and accounting teams
