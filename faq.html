Quick Answers

Answers for fleet owners, dispatchers, brokers, and accountants.

General

What is PDGI?

PDGI is a combined Fuel Portal and Transport Management System that centralizes fuel card management, weekly billing, load dispatch, driver mobile workflows, and invoicing for carriers and brokers.

Where do you operate?

We operate across Canada and the United States and support cross-border workflows (Canada ↔ USA).

Fuel Portal & Cards

How does weekly billing work?

Fuel transactions are grouped by account and aggregated into a single weekly invoice with line-item transactions and merchant details for reconciliation.

Can I set spending limits on cards?

Yes — set per-card controls like daily/weekly limits, allowed merchant categories and geo restrictions.

TMS & Driver App

Does the driver app work offline?

The app caches key data for offline use and syncs automatically when connectivity returns. Some features (live tracking) require network access.

Can drivers upload PODs from the app?

Yes — drivers can upload photos, attach signatures, and submit documents directly to the load record.
